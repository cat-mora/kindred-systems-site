About

I spent 20 years on the commercial side of business before I built anything with AI.

Marketing, sales, customer experience, CRM, growth. The measure of success was always the same: did revenue improve, did customers stay, did the team actually use what we built. Not whether the technology was impressive.

I founded and scaled an international business from the ground up. When COVID hit and the market shifted, I repositioned the business and led it through 400% revenue growth over two years, with a team of 21 people. We exited through acquisition. That experience taught me more about what makes change stick — and what kills it — than any tool or methodology.

When I started working with AI, I brought the same lens. What does the business need to achieve? What will the team actually adopt? What is the simplest path to a result that matters? The answers to those questions almost always come before the technology decision, not after it.

Through Kindred Systems, I work with businesses on both sides of that problem: advisory for organisations that need clearer thinking before they build, and managed AI solutions for businesses that want the outcome without the overhead of managing it themselves. I have built and deployed the systems I advise on — voice agents, automation workflows, lead-response and agent orchestration — so the advice is grounded in what AI can realistically do, not what vendors say it does.

Kindred Systems

What this business is and where it is going.

Kindred Systems is being built as a group of AI services and products that work together. The goal is a business that can operate independently of any single client — with recurring revenue, real products, and the capability to grow.

The advisory work

For mid-market and enterprise businesses that need someone between commercial and technical. Audits, strategy, implementation advisory and fractional AI leadership. The starting point is always what the business needs to achieve, not what AI can do.

The AI solutions

Managed AI products built and maintained by Kindred Systems. AI Assistant Pro handles calls, bookings and follow-ups for small service businesses. Digital Employee Pro — in development — is a broader AI working layer for established businesses.

What it does not do

Generic AI training, AI for its own sake, enterprise systems integration, or technology-first work before the business problem is understood. Engagements are declined when the fit is not right for both sides.

Where it is going

More products, more managed services, and over time, software that brings these capabilities to a wider market. A business that works — commercially, operationally, sustainably — built around the same principles it helps clients apply.
