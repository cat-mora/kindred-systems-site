About

I probably won't start with the fix you asked for.

Early in my career, I was hired to run marketing for a regional ferry company: brand refresh, campaigns, the usual brief. After a while observing how the business ran day to day, I could see the problem went deeper than marketing. With the owners' permission, I hired a secret shopping company to test the whole experience: calling to book, arriving at the terminal, boarding the vessel, and the crossing itself. That's where the real gaps were. Alongside the brand work, I introduced a loyalty program for locals, built campaigns to attract new audiences in the off-peak periods, hired a customer service trainer to work with all the staff, from booking staff through to onboard staff, and ran a media push that landed newspaper, magazine and television coverage, good enough that the Dalai Lama's team called after seeing it and asked us to host him on his next visit to Australia. The business grew to around 80% of the tightly held local market within a year. That's held true in every business I've worked with since: the fix people ask for is rarely the only fix needed.

It's held at scale too. I founded and scaled Change Empire Books into an international publishing and coaching business across Australia, the UK and the US. I was the business's primary revenue driver, personally closing inbound sales at over 80%, on programs priced from $5,000 to $95,000. When COVID hit, I repositioned the business, delivering 400% revenue growth over two years while leading a team of 21. The business exited through acquisition in 2023.

I've brought the same approach to contract work for other businesses: rebuilding a subscriber database of around 900,000 people and a nurture email sequence that lifted website visits 1,500% in its first month for one client, and running the social, email and SMS campaigns behind a large club's CRM to lift member visitation in a competitive local market for another.

For larger organisations, that usually means working alongside their own technical people rather than around them. Most already have an in-house IT team, and my role is helping decide where AI fits commercially and how it should work, while their team stays in control of the build. I'm currently completing certification in Microsoft's AI environments, since that's the platform most large and government organisations already run on.

Smaller businesses often don't have that in-house capability, and that's where Kindred Systems comes in: helping owners and leadership teams work out what's holding performance back and what to do about it.

More recently, I've worked at senior levels of government, which has given me a working understanding of the policy, procurement and governance considerations that shape decisions inside large organisations.

I work best with owners and leadership teams who want a straight answer about what's going on, even when it isn't the answer they expected.

IT managers often ask me what team I support but honestly, I stopped watching football when ET retired (IYKYK) and the short shorts that Capper used to wear turned me off AFL completely! So in my downtime, I am obsessed with working in my tropical-themed garden, walking my dog along the water, and optimistically booking back-to-back pilates classes.

Look forward to working with you soon

Cathryn

The advisory work

For mid-market and enterprise businesses that need someone between commercial and technical. Audits, strategy, implementation advisory and fractional AI leadership. The starting point is always what the business needs to achieve, not what AI can do.

What it does not do

Generic AI training, AI for its own sake, enterprise systems integration, or technology-first work before the business problem is understood. Engagements are declined when the fit is not right for both sides.

Where it is going

More products, more managed services, and over time, software that brings these capabilities to a wider market. A business that works commercially, operationally and sustainably, built around the same principles it helps clients apply.
