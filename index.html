Productised services

AI Assistant Pro and Digital Employee Pro.

For businesses that want a built, managed and ongoing AI solution rather than a consulting engagement, Kindred Systems offers two productised services. Both are designed, implemented and managed by Kindred Systems.

Natural-sounding voice agents

AI Assistant Pro

AI Assistant Pro is a voice-agent product for sole operators, tradies, clinics and small family-run service businesses that need phone conversations handled properly.

The missed-call setup answers or calls back, has a natural conversation, makes an appointment or transfers when needed. Rebooking and reactivation agents can work from customer records or calendars, identify people due for service, send email or SMS where useful, then call to book.

Clients choose the accent and speaking style so the voice suits the business, not a generic call centre.

Usually a setup fee, then around A$295 to A$795 per month.

More phone conversations, web enquiries and past customers become booked work, without the owner chasing every call, diary gap or reminder manually.

Managed AI employee systems

Digital Employee Pro

Digital Employee Pro gives established businesses multiple agents, managed as one AI employee system across sales, marketing, research, reporting, customer support, document handling, CRM tasks and internal operations.

A voice agent can be added where the business case supports it, but the main offer is a managed AI working layer that knows the business and improves over time.

Premium engagements are scoped around commercial value, with monthly retainers up to around A$5,000 per month.

More valuable work gets done across the business, without hiring another person for every operational gap or expecting leadership to manage AI tools and maintenance themselves.
