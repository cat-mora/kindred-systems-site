Product fit

Different businesses need different AI workers.

AI Assistant Pro and Digital Employee Pro are built for different buyers, different budgets and different commercial problems.

AI Assistant Pro is for small service businesses that need natural-sounding AI voice agents on the phone. The agent can answer calls, call leads back, speak with customers, book appointments, rebook past customers and escalate calls when a person needs to step in.

Digital Employee Pro is for established businesses that need multiple AI agents managed as one AI employee system across sales, marketing, research, reporting, customer support, documents, CRM and internal operations.

A pest control operator who needs annual rebooking calls is not the same buyer as an established firm that wants managed AI support across several parts of the business. Kindred Systems identifies where money, time or opportunity is being lost, then recommends the AI worker that makes commercial sense.

Natural-sounding voice agents

AI Assistant Pro

AI Assistant Pro is a voice-agent product for sole operators, tradies, clinics and small family-run service businesses that need phone conversations handled properly.

The missed-call setup is a voice agent that answers or calls back, has a natural conversation, makes an appointment or transfers/escalates when needed. Rebooking and reactivation agents stay voice-led, with extra capability to work from customer records or calendars, identify people due for service, send email or SMS where useful, then call to book.

Clients can choose the accent and speaking style, so the voice suits the business rather than sounding like a generic call centre bot.

Usually a setup fee, then around A$295 to A$795/month (approx US$210 to US$565/month).

More phone conversations, web enquiries and past customers become booked work, without the owner or their partner chasing every call, diary gap or reminder manually.

Managed AI employee systems

Digital Employee Pro

Digital Employee Pro gives established businesses multiple agents, managed as one AI employee system.

These agents usually work across desktop and business systems rather than on the phone: sales, marketing, research, reporting, customer support, document handling, CRM tasks and internal operations.

A voice agent can be added where it makes sense, but the main offer is a managed AI working layer across the business.

Premium engagements are scoped around the commercial value of the work, with monthly retainers up to around A$5,000/month (approx US$3,550/month).

More valuable work gets done across the business, without hiring another person for every operational gap or expecting the leadership team to manage AI tools, models and maintenance themselves.

Commercial lens

Kindred Systems starts with the business problem.

Some businesses lose money when the phone rings while the owner is on the tools, in appointments or driving between jobs. Some pay for ads or website traffic, then let form enquiries sit too long. Some have past customers who should be booked again, but nobody has time to chase the reminders.

AI Assistant Pro solves those problems with voice agents. The agent speaks to people, answers questions from the approved business information, books appointments, transfers urgent calls and can call past customers when they are due for another service.

Established firms usually have a different problem. Valuable work sits in inboxes, CRMs, documents, meeting notes, reports, support queues and people’s heads. Digital Employee Pro solves that with managed AI agents that work across software, documents and workflows.

Kindred Systems looks for the work that affects revenue, margin, follow-up, customer experience or operational drag, then builds the AI worker around the commercial result.

Product fit in plain English.

A small service business should not be sold a complex managed AI employee when it needs a natural-sounding voice agent to answer calls, call leads back, book appointments or rebook annual customers.

An established firm should not be limited to a basic phone agent when the bigger opportunity sits across sales operations, marketing operations, customer support, document work, reporting or internal workflows.

AI Assistant Pro

Best for sole operators, family-run and appointment-based service businesses where the return comes from phone conversations becoming booked work.

Think: the owner on the tools, in appointments or driving between jobs, while their partner is also trying to manage calls, reminders and diary gaps around their own work and life. AI Assistant Pro gives that business a voice agent that can speak to callers naturally, book, rebook or escalate.

  • Pest control annual reminders and rebooking
  • Air conditioning servicing and seasonal callbacks
  • Cleaning, pool, garden and maintenance services
  • Dental, skin, wellness and appointment businesses
  • Trades, urgent calls, quote requests and website leads

The return comes from more phone calls, form enquiries and past customers becoming booked work at a price that makes sense for a smaller business.

Digital Employee Pro

Best for established businesses where the value sits across complex workflows, executive leverage, sales operations, marketing operations, customer knowledge, reporting or internal systems. It is not voice-first, although a voice agent can be added if the business case is strong.

  • Law firms, insurance brokers and professional services firms with document-heavy workflows
  • Agencies, consultants and B2B service businesses with recurring delivery, client reporting or sales operations
  • Publishing, education or advisory businesses with enquiry-to-sale processes and repeatable fulfilment work
  • Real estate, finance, property and B2B firms with research, CRM, proposal or account-management workflows
  • Manufacturers, wholesalers and established teams needing AI support across sales, marketing, research, reporting, customer support or operations

The return comes from managed AI employees that know the business, work across its tools and improve over time, where the value can justify a higher monthly investment.
