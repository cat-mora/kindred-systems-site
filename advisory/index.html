About

Commercial first. Always.

My background is not technical in the traditional sense. For 20 years I worked across marketing, sales, customer experience, CRM, communications and growth. The measure of success was always commercial, not technical.

I founded and scaled an international business from the ground up, contributing to significant revenue growth before exiting through acquisition. That experience shapes how I approach AI work: I do not start with a favourite tool. I start with the outcome, the team, the constraints, and the reality of what adoption looks like in practice.

Through Kindred Systems, I advise established businesses on AI priorities, implementation decisions and adoption. The advisory work is grounded in a genuine understanding of what AI can realistically do, not what vendors and hype say it does.

I work best with founders, operations leaders and leadership teams who want someone who can see the full picture, ask the right questions, and help the right work get done in the right order.

Good to know

Before you enquire.

Practical details that answer most questions before they come up.

Pricing and GST

All prices are exclusive of GST. Australian clients are invoiced with 10% GST added. Invoices are issued per engagement milestone or monthly for retainers.

Travel and on-site work

Travel and accommodation for on-site engagements are charged at cost and agreed before work begins. Remote engagements incur no travel cost.

Internal sponsor

For multi-day and retainer engagements, clients need to nominate an internal sponsor with real decision-making authority. Engagements without this tend to stall.

Confidentiality

Confidentiality terms are available before substantive discovery begins. Client information is not shared with third parties or used across other engagements.

Retainer capacity

Fractional Growth & Systems Adviser slots are limited. Unused monthly capacity does not roll forward. Retained clients receive priority access for time-sensitive questions.
