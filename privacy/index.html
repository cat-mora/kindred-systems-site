Privacy

Privacy policy.

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Kindred Systems ("we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, how it is used, and who it is shared with.

What we collect

When you send an enquiry or book a call through this site, we collect the information you provide, which may include your name, email address, business name, and the details of what you are trying to solve. If you book a call, our scheduling provider also collects your phone number and preferred time.

How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry or confirm a booked call
  • To understand your business and prepare for a conversation with you
  • To follow up about Kindred Systems services you have asked about

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell or rent it to third parties.

Who we share it with

Enquiry forms on this site are processed by Formspree, a third-party form service, which forwards submissions to our email. Call bookings are handled by TidyCal, a third-party scheduling service. Both providers process your information on our behalf and have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

Cookies and tracking

This site does not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies. No analytics platform is currently active on this site.

Data storage

Enquiry and booking information is stored in our email and scheduling systems for as long as reasonably needed to respond to you and maintain business records, and is not kept beyond that without reason.

Your rights

You can ask us what information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it, by contacting us using the details below.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your information can be sent to info@kindredsystems.com.au.
