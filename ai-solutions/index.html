Digital Employee Pro

Digital Employee Pro is a managed AI employee system for established businesses that need more than a single agent handling one task. It is multiple AI agents, working together across different parts of the business, designed and managed as one coherent system.

Where AI Assistant Pro handles a specific job (phone calls and bookings), Digital Employee Pro gives the business an AI working layer that knows the business, handles more complex tasks, and improves over time.

Kindred Systems designs, builds and maintains the full system. You do not manage tools, prompts, models or maintenance. You define the outcomes you need and the system is built around those.

What the system can cover

  • Lead qualification and sales handling
  • Customer enquiry and support across your own documentation
  • Internal operations and reporting tasks
  • CRM data, follow-up and customer communications
  • Research, document handling and workflow automation
  • Marketing and content support
  • Voice agent layer where calls are part of the workflow
So that: more valuable work gets done across the business without hiring another person for every operational gap, or expecting leadership to manage AI tools and maintenance themselves.
